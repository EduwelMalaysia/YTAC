About the Event

The International Creativity & Innovation Award (ICIA) is the next stage of the Youth Tech Asia Challenge (YTAC), organized by Krya. It serves as an international platform where outstanding students from YTAC advance to compete alongside participants from countries such as the Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam, Sri Lanka, and Indonesia. At ICIA, students not only further refine their ideas and skills, but also gain valuable experience by collaborating and competing at a higher level. As one of the key pathways leading to the IITS global finals, ICIA plays a crucial role in preparing young innovators to take their solutions to the world stage.
