About Youth Tech Asia Challenge (YTAC)

Youth Tech Asia Challenge (YTAC) is an innovation-driven competition designed to empower young talents across Asia to explore coding, technology, engineering, and creativity. Each year, we gather top-performing students to compete, collaborate, and innovate solutions for the future.

【 YTAC 2025 】 MALAYSIA – KUCHING SARAWAK

An international competition founded by Eduwel, inviting students from Vietnam, Thailand, China, and Indonesia to compete and collaborate in Malaysia. Participants are tasked with proposing creative, tech-driven solutions under a unifying theme: Automation in the fields of Lifestyle & Security, Health, Transportation, and Education.

【 ICIA 2025 】 VIETNAM – HO CHI MINH

An international competition jointly organized by Eduwel and Shengxuan. Students must pass two qualifying rounds — YTAC and ICIA — by solving real-world challenges, earning their place in the ultimate showdown at IITS.

【 IITS 2025 】 CHINA – BEIJING

An international competition jointly organized by Eduwel and Shengxuan. Students must pass two qualifying rounds — YTAC and ICIA — by solving real-world challenges, earning their place in the ultimate showdown at IITS.
