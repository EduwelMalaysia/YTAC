About the Event

The International Innovation and Technology Showcase (IITS) is the final stage of the competition series, co-hosted by Sheng Xuan (China) and Eduwel. It is a prestigious international event that brings together top young innovators from across the globe to showcase their talents, creativity, and technological solutions.

Students who successfully advance through both YTAC and ICIA will be placed directly into the Primary or Secondary Competition Group, depending on their age category. In addition to these qualifiers, the organizers also invite students from various countries to participate in the Friendship Challenge — a special segment designed to encourage cross-cultural collaboration, friendship, and learning beyond the main competition.

IITS is more than just a competition — it is a global celebration of innovation, diversity, and the potential of youth on an international stage.

Categories

IITS Primary Group: aged 7 - 12 in year 2025

IITS Secondary Group: aged 13 - 18 in year 2025

Friendship Challenge: aged 7-21 in year 2025 (Under Special Invitations)
