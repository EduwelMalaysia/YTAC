About the Event

The Youth Tech Asia Challenge (YTAC) is an international innovation competition designed for students aged 7 to 18 who have a passion for coding and technology. This exciting event brings together young tech enthusiasts from different countries to not only compete, but also to exchange ideas, showcase their projects, and expand their horizons. What makes YTAC unique is its vibrant community — where students don’t just participate in a contest, but also learn from one another, explore creative solutions, and engage in meaningful discussions about each other’s work. It’s more than a competition — it’s a platform for tech-driven youth to connect, inspire, and grow together in a spirit of collaboration and innovation.
